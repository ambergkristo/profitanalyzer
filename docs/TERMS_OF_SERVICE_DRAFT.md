## Product Purpose

Profit Analyzer provides restaurant profit decision support. It helps review dish margins, ingredient costs, supplier price changes, invoice-derived cost updates, and pricing actions.

## Account And Workspace Responsibility

Users are responsible for controlling access to their workspace, inviting appropriate users, and keeping account details accurate.

## Data Accuracy

Users are responsible for reviewing menu, recipe, ingredient, supplier, invoice, OCR, and pricing data before relying on it. Incorrect input data can produce incorrect recommendations.

## OCR And Imported Data

OCR and imported invoice data must be reviewed before acting on it. OCR/upload creates a review draft only. Review-confirm is required before ingredient costs, cost history, or alerts update.
